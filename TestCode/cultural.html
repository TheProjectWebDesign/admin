Cultural Dance

The Maranao people possess a rich cultural heritage

in music, visual arts, and dance, including traditional dances like Singkil and Sagayan that convey courage and grace. These dances are imbued with symbolism from folklore and daily life, representing important narratives. Scholars have delved into the historical and cultural significance of Maranao dance. While adapting to modern trends, efforts are made to preserve Maranao dance traditions through cultural organizations and education. The dances reflect resilience, innovation, and cultural wealth, shaping identity and fostering community connections while honoring tradition and artistic excellence in Philippine culture.
